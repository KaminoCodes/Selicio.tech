What is COP26?

COP26 was the 26th climate change conference of the 'United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change', with the 'COP' meaning the 'conference of parties'[1].


COP History

The first Conference of Parties was held in 1994[2], where many world leaders joined the UNFCCC and agreed their contries would join the fight against Climate Change!
Since then a COP meeting has been held annually to discuss what their plans for the year are.


COP26 location

COP26 took place at the Scottish Events Campus (SEC) in Glasgow, Scotland, and was the biggest meeting of world leaders that the UK has ever hosted[3]
